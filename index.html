About me

Jie Song (宋杰) is currently pursuing his MSc at Beijing Normal University. He is interested in agricultural GHG emissions and food security. He developed methane (CH4) estimation approaches combined with machine learning and linear mixed model in order to characterize dynamics of CH4 emission from rice cultivation at a spatially-explicit resolution and explore the factors influencing emissions. Prof. Zhao Zhang is my mentor.
