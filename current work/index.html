Current Work

Now I'm leading a program on methane (CH4) emissions from rice cultivation

Phase 1: Data Collection

We focused on collecting field observations to compile the most comprehensive dataset on CH4 emissions from rice paddies. This dataset is the most extensive to date, containing a wide range of variables related to emission processes.

Phase 2: Modeling

We employed data-driven approaches (machine learning) and statistical methods (mixed-effects models) to establish the relationship between environmental conditions, management practices, and methane emissions in rice farming.

Phase 3: Data Analysis

We estimated the gridded CH4 emissions from global rice fields. We are exploring the spatiotemporal dynamics in emissions, identifying the key drivers, and developing strategies for emission mitigation.
